## Design Context

### Users
Chinese-speaking developers, product people, independent builders, and trend-watchers who regularly scan GitHub to discover, filter, compare, revisit, and analyze repositories.

### Brand Personality
Refined, calm, editorial, and quietly premium. The product should feel like a reading workspace for technical signal, not a noisy dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
A Readwise-like immersive reading desk adapted for GitHub intelligence: warm dark neutrals, strong bilingual typography, spacious card islands, low-noise controls, and contextual right-side drawers.

### Design Principles
- Reading-first hierarchy over dashboard density
- Excellent Chinese and English mixed typography
- Secondary controls recede until needed
- Context expands in drawers instead of interruptive centered modals
- Metrics support decision-making without overpowering title and summary
